# Waveform-Tools-using-PPT

A ray of sunshine for those who have auditory requirements such as strange fears about radios, auditory issues and trigonometry.

I've sought to ask questions about hearing loss and how many people wonder about the use of technology to correct or injure people with waveforms.  Most people are oblivious to an inaudible problem but modern tech is capable of helping people to determine the opportunity for future technology to automatically protect humans and animals from accoustic phenomenon.

Some have questions about the leaps forward with networks, devices and tools including AI.  But it would seem that each of us are responsible for ultimately protecting ourselves.  

This code offers an insight into how how those of us who see and feel energy can also reproduce it on screen and of course send responses.

What great about these is that they enable questions to be answered by a community of people working largely in health and science as well as trigonometry and engineering.  

The specifics of how these work are available online, in the file headers and also through asking questions about what it means to hear sound.

I'd like to thank everyone who is currently working on solving crime.
